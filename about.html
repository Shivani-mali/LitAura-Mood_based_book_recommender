📚 About LitAura

"A Book Recommender"

LitAura is a mood-based book recommender web app that helps users discover the perfect book based on how they’re feeling. It’s a creative blend of technology and emotional intelligence.

By using Sentiment Analysis, LitAura understands user moods like happy, calm, or thoughtful and then fetches relevant books using the Google Books API. Whether you’re seeking inspiration or comfort, LitAura gives you book suggestions that connect to your emotional state.

It’s not just a tool — it’s a personalized reading companion made to make book discovery smoother, smarter, and more meaningful.

🛠️ Built with HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, Sentiment.js & Google Books API

From the mood you type to the books you save — every part of LitAura is designed with purpose and passion. Let your mood pick your next favorite read. 💫
